The Anderson Music Hall holds a wide variety of music programs. Music business, music education, music performance, and music therapy are just a short list of the offered programs held within Anderson. Students also have the oppourtunity to join music group even if they are a non-music major, they can still take part in concert bands, symphony orchestras, jazz ensembles, and more.
