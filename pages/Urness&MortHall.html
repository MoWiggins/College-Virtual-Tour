Urness Tower and Mortensen

Urness Tower was built in 1966 and funded by Mr. and Mrs. Andrew Urness. It is specficially designed for first year students. Each floor of the tower has 18 rooms, a common area, and a community bathroom. Each floor also has a specific theme. For example, the third floor is for STEM leaders that identify as female. The tower also offers Wi-Fi, cable television, computer lab access, free laundry, and a skyway to Christensen. Each room includes a dresser, bed, closet, desk, and desk chair.
