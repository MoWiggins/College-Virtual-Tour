Urness & Mortenson Hall
Map More Information Home

Urness Hall is a 9 story dormatory that overlooks the city of Minneapolis. The residence hall is typically meant for first year residents. It is equipped with 18 rooms on each floor, a lounge space, as well as community bathroom. As for Mortenson Hall, it stands at 15 stories tall with 13 residential floors (8 dorms on each floor).
