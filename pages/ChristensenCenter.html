Christensen Center

The Christensen Center for vocation was built in 2001. It is named after Bernhard M. Christensen who was the 8th Augsburg President. The Christensen Center is the heart of the campus. Located in the Christensen Center is an Einsteins Bagels, and the Dinning hall for food. Additionally, there is the admissions office, the KAUG radio station, study abroad office, the Multicultural Sutdent Services office, and the Strommen Center for meaningful work.
