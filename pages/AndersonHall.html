Anderson Hall offers students a few options of the living styles for the school year:
Apartments: 4 students; includes a full bathroom, living room, and kitchen
Floorhouses: 15 students; includes a living room, dining room, full kitchen, and two bathrooms
Suites: 1 student per room; includes a shared bathroom, refrigerator, and microwave
Townhouses: 8 students/ 2 stories; includes a living room, dining room, full kitchen, and two bathrooms
