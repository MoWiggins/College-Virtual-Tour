The Lindell Library is a three story building that offers students a wide range of resources.
3rd Floor: Offers individual and group study spaces along with computers for coursework.
2nd Floor: Home to the CLASS Offices, TRIO Student Support Services, College Possible, Academic Advising, and the Academic Skills Office.
1st Floor: A learning commons that allows students to checkout books, movies, and even laptops. Basement: Known as the quiet floor where students can go if they seek peace and quiet when studying.
