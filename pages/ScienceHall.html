Science Hall
Map More Information Home

While the Science Hall is no longer the central space for the NorthStar STEM Program, it is still home to certain offices like the Equity and Inclusion Initiatives Office. The building also contains Augsburgs popular campus cupboard where students can stop by to donate or pick up local produce, clothes, and shoes.
