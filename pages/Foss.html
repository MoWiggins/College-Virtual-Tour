Foss Lobeck Miles Center

The Foss Lobeck Miles Center is home to the campus ministry. Additionally, the Theater department and Communications Sutdies and Film department call Foss their home. The main piece of Foss is the Hoversten chapel which includes a full organ and Steinway grand piano and which seats up to 650 guests and is used for Chapel, sunday gatherings, and convocation events.
