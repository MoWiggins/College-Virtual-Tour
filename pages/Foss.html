Foss Loebeck Miles Center
Map More Information Home

The Foss Loebeck Miles Center is home to the Campus Ministry, Theatre, Communication Studies, and Film majors. The multi-purpose building also houses the Hoversten Chapel which seats up to 650 guests and is used for Chapel, sunday gatherings, and convocation events.
