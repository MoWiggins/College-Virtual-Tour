Hagfors Center

The Hagfors Center for Science, Business, and Religion is our newest building on campus. It was completed in the January of 2018. Here is where most of our STEM majors will have a majority of their classes. It houses the Biology, Business, Chemistry, Computer Science, Physics, Psychology, Math, and Religion departments. There is a chapel on the third floor, a greenhouse on the fourth floor, a cafe on the second floor, and a plethora of classes and lab spaces. Each department has their own common area for students to study, or hangout. Hagfors is connected to the Lindell Library via skyway.
