The Kennedy Center is Augsburgs newest athletic facility. It is a multipurpose building that is 2 stories tall and has 3 floors.
Basement Floor: Contains a weight room and fitness center as well as locker room for Augsburg athletes.
Main Floor: A large gym that is used for various sports like baksetball, wrestling, baseball, etc. Top Floor: Classroom and lab spaces meant for students who have are studying exercise science.
