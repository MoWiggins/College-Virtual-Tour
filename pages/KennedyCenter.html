Kennedy Center

The Kennedy Center was completed in 2006. It it hosts training facilities and a fitness center. It is also home to the Athletic and Health and Physical Education departments.
Basement Floor: Contains a weight room and fitness center as well as locker room for Augsburg athletes.
Main Floor: A large gym that is used for various sports like baksetball, wrestling, baseball, etc.
Top Floor: Classroom and lab spaces meant for students who have are studying exercise science.
